ABOUT US

TeacherDirectory is a Pakistani portal, where you can find most a basic profile overview of most University teachers all over the nation. Information including courses taught, a basic description, profile pictures, everything is available on this website.

This is the first semester project of CS101 at DHA Suffa University. Designed and developed by Kumail Pirzada & Shayan Mustafa.
